# Architecture: Introduction

## Abstract

Introduction to the itenium Architecture track: what is a software architect, what kinds exist, what tasks they perform, and what good architecture means. Covers non-functional requirements, common pitfalls like over-engineering and ivory tower frameworks.

## Target Audience

Developers looking to understand the architect role and architectural thinking.

## Key Takeaways

- Understanding the different kinds of architects and how the role evolves from developer
- What makes good architecture and common pitfalls to avoid
- Overview of non-functional requirements that drive architectural decisions

## Session Format

60 minutes
